Request Account & Data Deletion

We respect your privacy. If you would like to delete your SynapseGuard account and have all of your personal data removed from our systems, please follow the steps below.

  1. Compose an email to our support team.
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request.
  3. Include the email address associated with your SynapseGuard account.
  4. Optionally, let us know the reason for your request (this helps us improve our service, but it is not required).
  5. Send the email to the address below.

📧 Send your deletion request to:  info@synapseguard.com

Once we receive your request, we will acknowledge it promptly and complete the deletion of your account and all associated personal data within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email when the deletion is complete.

Please note: Deleting your account is irreversible. All data associated with your account, including your profile, history, and preferences, will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.
